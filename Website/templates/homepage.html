The Concept

A bluetooth phonecase with mouse features built-in that works both as
a regular phone case and computer mouse, making it a convenient
accessory to carry-around in everyday lives.

The inspiration

Computer mouse is an essential item to designers. However, it tends to be built in bulk and is inconvenient to bring around. Resulting a minor burden to designers. Hence, the concept of the MouseCase aims to target this problem by merging the computer mouse into a phonecase as in this century our phones are always around us. Thus, introducing the concept of MouseCase 0.1.
